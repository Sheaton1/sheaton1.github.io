About Me

I am currently a 4th-year Ph.D. candidate at Zhejiang University, under the co-supervision of Prof. Yingcai Wu and Prof. Hui Zhang. I am studying at the State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University and a member of Interactive Data Group.

My research revolves around the intersection of computer and sports science. My work encompasses Sports Visual Analytics, Immersive Analytics, and Human-Computer Interaction.
